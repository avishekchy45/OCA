ONLINE CLASS ASSISTANT (OCA)


For Institutional Purposes:


It can be used to upload necessary documents, reference books, slides, videos by teachers. Enrolled student will be able to collect the documents from the OCA. Teachers can also upload marks, assign class works / assignment, schedule exams, announce notices (class postponed/ extra class) using OCA.

Why OCA?


- Teachers can easily inform student about class postpone. - Students can easily get necessary information, documents from course teacher. - And many more...

For Public Use:


Anyone who is expert in a particular topics can upload online courses (Paid/Free) as a video/slides/documents. Students can enroll to any courses and learn from online.

Why OCA?


- Teachers can share their valuable knowledge. - Students will get online assistance from online courses.
