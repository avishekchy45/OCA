ONLINE CLASS ASSISTANT (OCA)


For Institutional Purposes:

  • It can be used to upload necessary documents, reference books, slides, videos by teachers.
  • Enrolled students will be able to collect the documents from the OCA.
  • Teachers can also upload marks, assign class works / assignment, schedule exams, announce notices (class postponed/ extra class) using OCA.

Why OCA?

  • Teachers can easily inform student about class notices(such as class postponed/ extra class).
  • Students can easily get necessary information,documents in a common platform.
  • And many more...

For Public Use:

  • Anyone who is expert in a particular topics can upload online courses (Paid/Free) as a video/slides/documents.
  • Students can enroll to any courses and learn from online.

Why OCA?

  • Teachers can share their valuable knowledge.
  • Students will get online assistance from online courses.
  • And many more...
